Key Takeaways for Slidey.com Draft

Dan's Picks — The Exclusive Edge

No competitor offers a built-in analyst prediction tracker with live scoring. Dan's Picks gives Slidey a content moat: users follow Dan's analysis pre-draft, then watch his accuracy unfold in real-time on draft night. The "Beat Dan" challenge turns passive readers into engaged competitors.

Exclusive Content moat Engagement driver

The Gap to Own

No app nails the full friend-vs-friend draft night experience. MockOut is closest on mobile, AIMockDraft has the broadest feature set, but neither combines private pools + rich scoring + live draft tracking + analyst content seamlessly. This is Slidey's lane.

Opportunity

Must-Have Features (MVP)

Private pools with invite codes, pre-draft mock submission with lock deadline, tiered scoring engine, live draft-night scoreboard, Dan's Picks analyst tracker, and social sharing of results.

Pool system Scoring engine Live tracker Dan's Picks

Full Content Platform

Slidey goes beyond prediction games with a full editorial stack: Big Board prospect rankings, scouting reports, team needs pages, combine measurables, and a mock draft aggregator. This positions Slidey as a destination, not just a game.

Big Board Scouting reports Team intel Aggregator

Simulator Engine

A light mock draft simulator for internal use that grows over time: solo drafting against AI teams, 7-round depth, trade engine, pick grading, and saved draft archives. Keeps users engaged between draft seasons.

Solo sim Trade engine Pick grades

Differentiators to Consider

Trade prediction bonuses (MockOut's 20pt bonus is compelling), prop-style side bets, position-rank scoring (nobody does this well), AI-powered analysis, and year-over-year accuracy profiles for both users and Dan.

Trade bonuses Prop bets AI-powered Accuracy profile
