★ Neon / Vercel Postgres

Best fit for Slidey.com Draft. Zero-config Vercel integration, no inactivity deletion, free branching for dev/prod. You only miss built-in auth and real-time — neither needed for a draft board.

Best for: Vercel-deployed apps, seasonal/low-traffic projects, serverless architectures

Supabase

Most feature-rich option — auth, real-time, storage, REST APIs all included. But the inactivity deletion on free tier is a dealbreaker for seasonal apps unless you pay $25/mo year-round.

Best for: Apps needing full BaaS (auth + real-time + storage), active daily-use projects

Turso

Impressive free tier (5 GB, 500 DBs) with no inactivity risk. Edge-native reads are blazing fast. But SQLite means no easy migration to/from Postgres, and the ecosystem is younger.

Best for: Read-heavy apps, edge performance, projects that won't need Postgres compatibility

Railway

Solid managed Postgres with simple DX, but no real free tier — $5/mo minimum. Good as a secondary option or for apps that already live on Railway's platform.

Best for: Full-stack deploys (app + DB in one platform), teams already on Railway
